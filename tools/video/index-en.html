03 Architecture

Six packages. Two services. One chain.

Every layer is small, tested, and replaceable. Each does one job well.

policy

workflow
spec → DAG compile
registry

routing

DAG walk + retry + HITL
verifier

Write and financial operations pause for a human-in-the-loop approval. Disagreements between verifiers trigger bounded retries. The receipt is the contract.
